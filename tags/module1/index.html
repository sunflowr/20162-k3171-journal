Project Description from Fredrik In the 2D-illustration the space is created between the objects ”balls”. However the idea of the balls are dependent on the viewers perspective and relation to its surrounding. If you don’t have any relation to a object you will compare it to similar objects. The balls can be viewed upon two ways. That the balls are the same size and they will seam to be more or less distant from the viewers point of view.
